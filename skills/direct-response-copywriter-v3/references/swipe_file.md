## 148. Copywriting Principle — "Don't Personalize Negativity"

> **Rule:** Never personalize negative statements directly to the reader. Use third-person framing for negative/problem content, then switch to personalized "you" language only for the positive/solution content.
>
> **Wrong (negatively personalized):** "Once you turn 35, your hormones change and when that happens, your skin's elasticity decreases. And this is why, your skin can't shrink as much as the scale shrinks in numbers."
>
> **Correct (not negatively personalized):** "Once a woman turns 35, hormones change and when that happens skin's elasticity decreases. And this is why skin can't shrink as much as the scale shrinks in numbers."
>
> **Then switch to positively personalized:** "Now here's the good news, it's possible for you to tighten and tone loose skin by giving your skin everything it needs to increase elasticity. And when you start doing this, you'll enable your skin to shrink itself."
