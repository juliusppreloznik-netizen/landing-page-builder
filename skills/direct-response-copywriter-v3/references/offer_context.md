# Offer Context: The Client Attraction Funnel

This document contains the complete details of the core offer, "The Client Attraction Funnel." It is the source of truth for all product-related claims, features, and outcomes.

---

## 1. Core Offer Summary

-   **Product Name:** The Client Attraction Funnel
-   **Company:** Catalyst Marketing
-   **Service:** A done-for-you client acquisition system for credit repair and funding business owners.
-   **Price Range:** $2,500 - $10,000
-   **Core Transformation:** Go from inconsistent, unpredictable client flow (or no clients) to a predictable, scalable system that generates 5-12 qualified sales calls per day.

---

## 2. The Unique Mechanism: The Four Pillars

The Client Attraction Funnel is a proprietary system built on four integrated pillars. This is the Unique Mechanism.

| Pillar | What It Is | Why It Matters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Market Repositioning** | A strategic framework that reframes commoditized "credit repair" as a high-value, specialized solution (e.g., "The Investment Property Approval System"). | Breaks out of price competition, creates a category of one, and allows for premium pricing ($2K-$4K vs. $300-$500). |
| **2. Paid Advertising System** | An AI-powered Facebook advertising system using deep market research, multiple ad angles, and advanced pixel conditioning. | Provides the only scalable method to reach thousands of qualified prospects daily with predictable cost-per-acquisition. Overcomes the ceiling of organic marketing. |
| **3. Authority Positioning Funnel** | A Direct-to-Application (DTA) funnel with a VSL and conditional logic that pre-qualifies and pre-sells prospects before they book a call. | Filters out unqualified leads automatically, builds authority, and ensures sales teams only speak with high-intent, budget-qualified prospects. |
| **4. Backend Selling System** | An automated post-booking nurture system (email + SMS) that uses case studies and reminders to keep prospects engaged and increase show rates. | Drives show rates from the industry standard of 30-40% up to 65-75%, ensuring booked calls turn into actual sales conversations. |
