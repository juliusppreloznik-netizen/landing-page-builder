## 2. Winning Ad Analysis

### A. Dominant Hooks & Angles

| Hook / Angle | Description | Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Specific Claim w/ Proof** | Open with a big, specific revenue or lead claim and immediately show proof. | "This AI lead generation strategy...made my credit per business 75 k in one month. And here's the proof." (CID 12) |
| **Challenge Common Wisdom** | Call out the audience and tell them to stop doing what they've been told (posting content, DMs). | "If you run a credit per business and haven't hit fifty ks months yet, stop doing this." (CID 2) |
| **Market Size / Inevitability** | Frame the market as huge and their lack of success as an anomaly caused by being invisible. | "There are 140 million people in America with poor credit right now...So why aren’t you at $30k/month yet?" (CID 6) |
| **Simple ROI Question** | Open with a simple, self-evident math question that frames the service as an investment. | "Would you pay $65 to get a client that pays you 500, a thousand, even 1,500 for your service?" (CID 1) |
| **Take-Away / Exclusivity** | Use a headline that implies high demand and filters for serious prospects only. | "Don’t apply if you can’t handle more credit repair clients" (CID 19) |
| **Speed & Immediacy** | Promise a specific number of calls in a very short timeframe. | "...you could have 7,10, even 20 sales calls booked directly on your calendar just 48 hours later." (CID 5) |

### B. Common Ad Structure (The "Catalyst Formula")

1.  **The Hook:** Grab attention using one of the dominant angles above.
2.  **Agitate the "Old Way":** Call out the pain and unpredictability of organic marketing (posting content, sending DMs, relying on referrals).
3.  **Introduce the "New Way":** Position the "Client Attraction Funnel" as a predictable system that replaces manual effort.
4.  **Explain the Mechanism:** Briefly explain *how* it works (Facebook AI to find qualified/funded prospects, VSL to pre-sell, application to qualify).
5.  **Provide Proof:** State specific results (e.g., "$65 into $1,500", "75k in one month", "5-10 qualified leads per day").
6.  **State the Offer:** Clearly say, "We'll build the whole entire thing for you."
7.  **Risk Reversal:** Offer a strong guarantee ("or we'll refund you every single penny or work for free until we do").
8.  **Call to Action:** "Click the button below to book a call."
